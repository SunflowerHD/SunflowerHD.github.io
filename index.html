Research
Deep Neural Networks for solving American put option under the Black-Scholes model.

Area: Machine Learning, numerical PDEs | Supported by NSFC

  • Design Full connected Deep Neural Networks with special multigrid and cost function to replace classical Finite Element Method in American put option pricing problem.
  • Simulation of DNNs on datasets in 2-dimention PDEs of BS equations with Admm and SGD to optimize.Comparison with Binomial tree method shows accurate results with 0.6% relative error.
  • Future opportunities: design fast and potential NNs for high-dimentional PDEs, especially in option pricing and inverse problems in which classical methods meet the trouble of Curse of Dimensionality.
